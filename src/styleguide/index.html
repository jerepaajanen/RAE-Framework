One Morning, When Gregor Samsa Woke

From troubled dreams, he found himself transformed in his bed into a horrible vermin. He lay on his armour-like back, and if he lifted his head a little he could see his brown belly, slightly domed and divided by arches into stiff sections. The bedding was hardly able to cover it and seemed ready to slide off any moment.

His many legs, pitifully thin compared with the size of the rest of him, waved about helplessly as he looked. “What’s happened to me?” he thought. It wasn’t a dream. His room, a proper human room although a little too small, lay peacefully between its four familiar walls.

Modular Scale

Instead of trying to maintain a baseline grid, RAE favors a modular scale and unit-less line heights

Here are few common scale factors:
  • Major Second 1.125
  • Minor Third 1.200
  • Major Third 1.250
  • Perfect Fourth 1.333
  • Augmented Fourth 1.414
  • Perfect Fift 1.500
  • Golden Ratio 1.618

List of mixins
Mixin Result

.vertically-centered(); Vertical align anything, even if you don’t know elements height

.momentun-scrolling(); Add momentum scrolling to element
